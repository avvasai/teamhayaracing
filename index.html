Introduction

PES Univesity, located in Bangalore, India is one of the country’s leading teaching and research univesities. The Univesity is committed to providing “education for the real world” that inspires student to realize their potential. Our students graduate with the ability to adapt to an intellectually and technologically changing evironment. Team HAYA, the racing car team of PES University actively participates in Supra SAE, Enduro Student India, Baa SAE, Formula Bharath and major competitons on a national and international level.

Goals

The team strives to break its own barriers at every step, to reach new heights of development, and to improve with every stride. The Team aims to achieve the following milestones within a duration of three years: Overall winners of Formula Bharat and SUPRA SAE by 2021. Participate in an overseas Formula Student event by 2021. Shift the team's participation from domestic to exclusively international by 2022.
